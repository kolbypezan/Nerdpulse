Community Guidelines

To keep our community welcoming and productive, please follow these simple rules:

  • Be Respectful: Treat everyone with kindness. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
  • Stay On Topic: Keep discussions related to Bitcoin, mining, NerdMiners, and our community.
  • No Spam or Excessive Self-Promotion: Share relevant links, but avoid spamming.
  • Share Knowledge: Help others learn! If you know something, share it.
  • No Financial Advice: This is a space for learning and hobbyists, not for investment advice.
  • Keep it Legal: Don't share or promote illegal activities.

Admins and moderators reserve the right to remove content or members who violate these guidelines.
